### Haansoft (bundled with Hancom Office & Thinkfree Office)

These fonts have white space in PSName.

[WARNING]
These fonts are also available as part of free viewers
"Thinkfree Office viewer" and "HWP Viewer", but the usage of fonts
is restricted by license specification.
On Windows, `HBATANG.TTF` and `HDOTUM.TTF` are installed under
`C:\Program Files (x86)\Hnc\Office NEO\HOfficeViewer96\Shared\TTF\All`.
On Ubuntu, these two (together with HCR families) are installed under
`/usr/share/hnc/hoffice9/viewer/hwpviewer/Shared/TTF/All`.
These fonts are licensed only for use with Hancom Office/Thinkfree Office,
so it is forbidden to use these fonts with TeX/Ghostscript.
(although font files do not have any restriction for embedding)

- Website: http://www.hancom.com/en/product/office_viewer.jsp
    - Windows installer: `HOffice_NEO_Viewer.exe`
    - Ubuntu package: `hwpviewer_9.20.0.346_i386.deb`
- License information: http://www.hancom.com/notice.noticeView.do?notice_seqno=203
